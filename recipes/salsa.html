D's Famous Salsa

Description

D’s Famous Salsa is a simple yet flavorful Texas-style salsa that has become a go-to recipe for many home cooks. Developed by Denise Smith, this salsa combines pantry staples to create a zesty dip that's ready in just 10 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 2 (14.5 ounce) cans stewed tomatoes
  • ½ onion, finely diced
  • ¼ cup canned sliced green chiles, or to taste
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • ½ lime, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Place tomatoes, onion, green chiles, cilantro, lime juice, garlic, and salt in a blender or food processor. Blend on low to desired consistency.
